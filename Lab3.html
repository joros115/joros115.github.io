BIO

I love the Outdoors, I am a student at University of Wisconsin Madison studing GIS, living in Madison WI, I hold two undergraduate degrees from University of Wisconsin Stevens Point in Biology and Fisheries/ Waters research. My background includes natural resource conservation, land use following best management practices, fish propagation and manure discharge effects into public water bodies. My interests for the future includes fish migration/movement patterns, bathymetric maps, large water body navigation as it relates to climate change, land use affected by frac sand mining and environmental changes to aquatic species or invasive species as it relates to global climate change. Thanks for taking the time to get to know me.
